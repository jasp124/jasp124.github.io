About

My name is Jasper, and I’m a 16-year-old passionate about football and photography. I play for Tankerton U18s, where I’ve developed my skills and love for the game. Being part of a team has taught me the importance of teamwork, discipline, and dedication, both on and off the field. In addition to football, I’m currently studying computer science, with the goal of pursuing a degree in this field at university. I’ve always been fascinated by technology and how it shapes our world. I enjoy the challenges that come with coding and problem-solving, and I’m excited about the endless possibilities that a career in computer science can offer. Photography is another passion of mine. I mainly focus on capturing nature and cityscapes, using my camera to explore the beauty around me. I find inspiration in the little details of the world, whether it’s a stunning sunset, the intricate patterns of leaves, or the dynamic energy of urban life. Photography allows me to express my creativity and appreciate the world from different perspectives. Balancing football, academics, and photography keeps me busy, but I enjoy every moment. I’m looking forward to the future, whether it’s on the pitch, in a computer lab, or out in nature with my camera. I believe that my experiences in sports and my studies will shape me into a well-rounded individual, ready to take on whatever challenges lie ahead.
